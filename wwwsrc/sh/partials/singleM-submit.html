Journal Theme

期刊主题

In Search of the Most Serious Absurdity and the Most Honest Fiction

寻找最严肃的荒诞与最诚实的虚构

FAKE is now officially accepting submissions from scholars, researchers, and academic enthusiasts worldwide!

FAKE 现已正式向全球学者、研究人员及学术爱好者开放征稿！

We are dedicated to establishing a premier satirical journal characterized by an "impeccably formal exterior, utterly absurd content, and crystal-clear ethical boundaries." Here, we wrap the most preposterous academic conclusions in a highly rigorous scholarly facade to deconstruct and satirize the various dysfunctions of contemporary academia.

我们致力于打造一本具有“无可挑剔的正式外表、彻底荒谬的内容以及清晰明确的道德边界”的顶级讽刺期刊。在这里，我们用高度严谨的学术外衣包裹最荒谬的学术结论，以解构并讽刺当代学术界的种种异化现象。

我们的使命：

一本用虚构的发现揭示真实问题的期刊。

核心原则：

高贵的格式。荒谬的内容。诚实的免责声明。

Submission Red Lines & Ethical Boundaries

投稿红线与道德边界

Before you begin writing, you must familiarize yourself with and adhere to FAKE’s ironclad rules:

在您开始撰稿之前，必须熟悉并遵守 FAKE 的铁律：

Strictly Prohibited: Tutorials on Actual Academic Misconduct
We do not accept manuscripts that teach readers how to evade institutional oversight or manipulate data in real life. We only satirize the underlying logic of academic fraud.

严格禁止：真实学术不端行为的教程
我们不接受教授读者如何在现实生活中规避机构监管或操纵数据的稿件。我们仅讽刺学术造假的底层逻辑。

Anticipated Submission Types & Examples

预期稿件类型与示例

To help authors better align with our editorial direction, we have outlined the five permanent pillar columns of FAKE, along with a series of illustrative topics for reference:

为了帮助作者更好地契合我们的编辑方向，我们列出了 FAKE 的五个常设支柱栏目，并提供了一系列示例选题以供参考：

Research Articles

研究文章 (Research Articles)

  • Column Overview: The main course of the journal. Submissions must fully adhere to the formal structure of top-tier journals like Nature or Science (Abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion). They should employ an incredibly serious, sophisticated academic tone and argumentation to derive an entirely preposterous conclusion. 栏目概述： 本刊的正餐。投稿必须完全遵循 Nature 或 Science 等顶刊的正式结构（摘要、引言、方法、结果、讨论）。应采用极其严肃、深奥的学术口吻和论证，来推导出一个完全荒谬的结论。
  • Anticipated Directions: 预期方向：
    • Article / Letter: Full-length or short, high-concept papers featuring mock discoveries. Article / Letter： 包含模拟发现的完整长文或高概念短文。
    • Synthetic Data Experiments: Specifically designed to satirize data manipulation and statistical misuse (e.g., p-hacking, selective exclusion of outliers). Note: Editors may append a notice stating, "All data in this article is synthetic and intended to satirize common statistical misuses; it does not constitute actionable data-processing advice." 合成数据实验： 专为讽刺数据操纵和统计滥用（如 p值操纵、选择性剔除异常值）而设计。注：编辑可能会附加声明：“本文中的所有数据均为合成数据，旨在讽刺常见的统计滥用；不构成可操作的数据处理建议。”
  • Sample Topics: 示例选题：
    • Achieving Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) Through Model Renaming — Conclusion: When a model's name includes "Omni-Ultra-Reasoning," its performance exhibits a statistically significant, cliff-like surge. 通过模型重命名实现通用人工智能 (AGI) —— 结论：当模型名称包含“Omni-Ultra-Reasoning”时，其性能表现出统计学上显著的断崖式暴涨。
    • The Existence of Dragons Reaches Statistical Significance Following Selective Outlier Exclusion (A satire on data manipulation). 选择性剔除异常值后恐龙的存在性达到统计学显著水平（讽刺数据操纵）。
    • CRISPR-Based Graduate Student Personality Editing — Conclusion: Upon deleting the procrastination gene, subjects spontaneously begin drafting the Introduction section of their manuscripts. 基于 CRISPR 的研究生性格编辑 —— 结论：在删除拖延基因后，受试者开始自发撰写稿件的引言部分。

News & Comment

新闻与评论 (News & Comment)

  • Column Overview: Shorter, opinion-driven pieces focusing on current academic hot topics. This column is ideal for articulating the absurdities of academia using an impeccably straight-faced journalistic tone, offering high potential for viral sharing. 栏目概述： 聚焦当前学术热点、由观点驱动的较短篇幅文章。该栏目非常适合用一本正经的新闻报道口吻来阐述学术界的荒谬现象，具有极高的病毒式传播潜力。
  • Anticipated Directions: Editorial, News Briefing, Correspondence, Retraction Watch Theatre. 预期方向： 社论 (Editorial)、新闻简报 (News Briefing)、读者来信 (Correspondence)、撤稿观察剧场 (Retraction Watch Theatre)。
  • Sample Topics: 示例选题：
    • News Briefing: New Study Shows Significant Correlation Between Reviewer 2’s Comments and Lunar Phases 新闻简报： 最新研究显示审稿人2的评议意见与月相之间存在显著相关性
    • Retraction Watch Theatre: Pre-Retraction Science: This Paper Has Not Yet Been Retracted, But the Authors Remain Optimistic 撤稿观察剧场： 预撤稿科学：本文尚未被撤稿，但作者依然保持乐观
    • Correspondence: Author Response: We Did Not Exaggerate the Statistical Significance of the Tyrannosaurus Rex 读者来信： 作者回应：我们没有夸大暴龙的统计学显著性

Reviews & Analysis

综述与分析 (Reviews & Analysis)

  • Column Overview: Geared toward authors with deeper domain expertise who can systematically deconstruct academic culture or map out a hypothetical field of study. 栏目概述： 面向具有更深专业领域知识的作者，能够系统地解构学术文化或勾勒出一个虚构的研究领域。
  • Anticipated Directions: Review Article (systematically mapping out a non-existent field), Perspective (insightful critiques of dysfunctional academic cultures), Analysis (satirical analysis of academic anomalies using public data). 预期方向： 综述文章 (Review Article，系统性地勾勒一个不存在的领域)、透视 (Perspective，对异化学术文化的深刻批判)、分析 (Analysis，利用公开数据对学术异常现象进行讽刺性分析)。
  • Sample Topics: 示例选题：
    • A Review of Quantum Necromancy: From Entangled Emotions to Non-Local Peer Review 量子通灵术综述：从纠缠的情感至非定域同行评议
    • The "Mechanism Hero Diagram" Culture in Modern Experimental Sciences 现代实验科学中的“机制英雄图”文化
    • How Beautiful Figures Outlive Unbeautiful Data 美丽的图表如何比不美丽的数据活得更久

Peer Review Theatre

同行评议剧场 (Peer Review Theatre)

  • Column Overview: The column with the highest viral potential. We warmly invite submissions that share, adapt, or invent peer review comments and author rebuttals that are either infuriating or hilarious. 栏目概述： 最具病毒式传播潜力的栏目。我们热忱欢迎投稿分享、改编或虚构那些令人气愤或捧腹的同行评议意见及作者反驳。
  • Anticipated Directions: Reviewer 2 Reports (creative writing inspired by Reviewer 2), Author Response (mock author rebuttals), Editorial Decision (mock editorial decision letters). 预期方向： 审稿人2报告 (Reviewer 2 Reports，受审稿人2启发的创意写作)、作者回应 (Author Response，模拟作者反驳)、编辑决定 (Editorial Decision，模拟编辑决定信)。
  • Sample Topics: 示例选题：
    • Reviewer Comment: "Reviewer #2: This manuscript holds potential significance, but the authors should replicate all experiments in a parallel universe." 审稿人意见： “审稿人#2：这篇手稿具有潜在意义，但作者应该在平行宇宙中复制所有实验。”
    • Reviewer Comment: "We suggest the authors replicate this experiment in a larger mammal, preferably a blue whale." 审稿人意见： “我们建议作者在更大的哺乳动物中复制该实验，最好是蓝鲸。”

Archives & Awards

档案与奖项 (Archives & Awards)

  • Column Overview: The journal's annual entertainment and awards section, open to nominations or parodic submissions. 栏目概述： 本刊的年度娱乐与颁奖板块，接受提名或恶搞投稿。
  • Anticipated Directions: Best Figure Manipulation, Most Elegant Null Result Disappearance, and the Lifetime Achievement Award for Unverifiability. 预期方向： 最佳图表操纵奖、最优雅无效结果消失奖，以及不可验证性终身成就奖。

Special Call for Papers: Inaugural Issue Themes

特刊征稿：创刊号主题

If your creative concepts align with any of the following five special issues for our inaugural edition, please indicate so upon submission:

如果您的创意构想与我们创刊号的以下五个特刊中的任何一个相契合，请在投稿时注明：

  • Special Issue I: Evidence for Evidence
    Dedicated to papers that prove no substantive conclusions but exhaust every effort to demonstrate that "there appear to be indications," satirizing over-interpretation.
    Example: Sign of a Sign of Life on Mars: Indirect Evidence for a Possible Feature of a Potential Mechanism     特刊 I：证据的证据 (Evidence for Evidence)
    专门接收未能证明任何实质性结论，但竭尽全力论证“似乎存在迹象”的论文，用以讽刺过度解读。
    示例：火星生命迹象的迹象：潜在机制之可能特征的间接证据
  • Special Issue II: Beautiful Figures, Fragile Data
    Targeting "figure aesthetics literature," including mechanistic diagrams, stitched micrographs, and overly smoothed data plots.
    Example: A Unified Mechanism Model Diagram Capable of Explaining Everything Except the Data     特刊 II：绝美配图，脆弱数据 (Beautiful Figures, Fragile Data)
    针对“配图美学文献”，包括机制图、拼接的显微照片以及过度平滑的数据图表。
    示例：一个能解释除数据以外任何事情的统一机制模型图
  • Special Issue III: The Reviewer 2 Collection
    A dedicated compilation of the most absurd, pedantic, and impossible peer-review feedback.     特刊 III：审稿人2文集 (The Reviewer 2 Collection)
    专门汇编最荒谬、迂腐且不可能实现的同行评议反馈。
  • Special Issue IV: Synthetic Proofs of Impossible Claims
    Example: Weakly Significant Evidence for the Immortality Hypothesis in a Cohort of Highly Proactive Mice     特刊 IV：不可能声称的合成证明 (Synthetic Proofs of Impossible Claims)
    示例：在一组高度积极的小鼠中支持永生假设的弱显著证据
  • Special Issue V: Retraction-Ready Science
    Articles published complete with their own pre-packaged mock institutional investigations or mock retraction notices.     特刊 V：开箱即撤型科学 (Retraction-Ready Science)
    发表时即自带预先包装好的模拟机构调查报告或模拟撤稿通知的文章。
