How to Submit Manuscript

如何投稿

Use the Article Starter Kit to organize files and directories. When you are ready, put necessary files into a ZIP archive, and upload it here. Make sure to exclude files that are not directly shown to readers, e.g. raw data files and training sets.

Alternatively ways of submission include:

  1. sending email to submission (at) fakejournal.org
  2. creating an issue on GitHub
